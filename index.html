Code Result Task: For SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation), a household uses 250 liters of water per day. If a new water-efficient system reduces usage by 40 liters per day, how much water will be saved in 30 days?
old_usage = 250
new_usage = old_usage - 40
days = 30
water_saved = (old_usage - new_usage) * days
print(water_saved)
 -> What is the total water saved after 30 days?
